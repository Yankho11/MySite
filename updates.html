Welcome to the update log!

Updated - 31 June 2024

  • Added video showcasing dynamic lighting in Helix and free camera

    • It's me, back again with another update
    (I actually had one ready at the beginning of this month because I planned to do updates bi-weekly
    but unfortunately I couldn't find the time and in the chaose I broke the engine, but here we are).
    So I've been working on getting dynamic lighting into the engine but that got halted when I accidentally
    broke the camera systems for the engine. Things were kind of all over the show with the original build of the helix
    and in my attempt to get things into the engine quickly due to my lack of time, I ended up breaking the camera instead
    making rendering unpredictable. I kinda got defeated by this and just didn't touch the engine for a while, but after
    coming accross some free time and playing some games I remembered why I love compputer graphics and decided to reuild
    the engine from scratch modularising/factorising things out as effectively as possible while trying to cater to performance
    and resource allocation. The final result of 2 weeks of work is all in the uploaded video. I actualy do have a texture loader
    set up in the engine as well and I'll upload a video showcasing that as well.


    What's next for Helix?
    As of now I'm planning on adding the ability select different materials for objects as well as setting up
    HDR.
